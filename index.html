About Us

PAPER TO BAG IS A COMPANY BASED OUT OF CHENNAI, INDIA THAT DEALS WITH PAPER PRODUCTS

Growing up, I have always felt artistically inclined when it came to my hobbies or anything else. An unexpected break during my last year of undergraduation made me think “Why not make paper bags and sell them?” With just a Facebook page and a few orders from people I knew personally, “Paper to Bag” kickstarted.


That epiphany happened in 2013. From then till now, “Paper to Bag” has come a long way. What started off as a one-woman army who sold just paper bags, has now grown to an entire team that caters to weddings, birthday parties, bridal showers, baby showers and customized gifting solutions as well.


Customization and personalization are key when it comes to our products. We cater to the needs of the clients and spend quality time with them to understand their expectations before we get down to designing and handcrafting.Our varied clientele includes end-users, small and medium businesses, corporatesand event managers.


It has been an exciting journey so far and we can’t wait to see what more awaits us in the years to come!

All of 22, Zeba A. Rahman, knew she never wanted to settle for a regular job. Which is why, she launched Paper to Bag, a customised party supplies retailer, while in her final year of college in 2013. “I was always involved in social activities. In my third year, we suddenly had a 20-day break due to a strike. I didn’t want to waste my time. That’s how Paper to Bag came about,” she says. Read more

Conflating Visions

Zeba A Rahman was only 12-years-old when she along with one of her friends gave shape to her first business model. And, the product they sold was a few plain looking pebbles. They painted the pebbles with different colours and made them look attractive. The smart thinking yielded productive results as the pebbles got sold in quick time. In 2014, Zeba started Paper to Bag, an entrepreneurial venture based in Chennai. As the name suggests, Paper to Bag started with recycling papers to create beautiful bags.
